🛡 Collaborative Points Philosophy
ℹ

Why split points into 'Combined' and 'Individual'?

The points are split to foster a collaborative environment rather than a competitive one. By separating Combined and Individual categories, we prevent friction between Chapter Leaders within the same chapter.

Combined Points: Awarded to all active Chapter Leaders who contribute to the community in that quarter.

Individual Points: Points each Chapter Leader earns independently through event promotion, referrals, and engagement.
